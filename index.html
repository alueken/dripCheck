What problem are you solving?

Getting dressed should be fun, not stressful. Drip Detector uses a Teachable Machine image model to give playful feedback on how strong your outfit is in real time using your webcam.

This project is designed for high school students who:

  • Want quick outfit feedback before school or events
  • Like experimenting with style and seeing how AI reads it
  • Are curious about how machine learning can spot clothing patterns
Tech: Teachable Machine ¬∑ TensorFlow.js
Division: Technology ¬∑ Art

How to use the Drip Detector

  1. Make sure you are in good lighting and your top, bottom, and outerwear are visible.
  2. Click Start Drip Check and allow the browser to use your camera.
  3. Stand back so your outfit fits in the frame and hold a natural pose.
  4. Read your drip rating and message on the right side.
  5. Try different outfits and see how the predictions change.

Model details: This Teachable Machine model has at least three classes: Nice Jacket, Nice Shorts, Nice Sweatshirt/Crewneck, Undetected, Nice Pants, Nice Shirt, Bad Fit. The website runs the exported model fully in the browser.
